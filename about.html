About us

The Edmonton Dragons Basketball Club (EDBC) was founded in 2016, by a group of basketball fanatics. As a non-profitable organization, the EDBC focus on providing ultimate services to all basketball enthusiasts. Whether you simply want to play some pickup games for fun, or further challenge yourself to a higher level of basketball (the EBA), EDBC is always a good choice for you.
