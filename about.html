About us

The Edmonton Dragons Basketball Club (EDBC) was founded in 2016, by a group of basketball fanatics. As a non-profitable organization, the EDBC focus on providing ultimate services to all basketball enthusiasts. Whether you simply want to excercise the body to improve your health, or further challenge yourself to a higher level of basketball, the EBA, EDBC is always a good choice for you. Club rosters consists of professionals from different industries, engaing with different activities in the club is also a great way to extend your social networking
